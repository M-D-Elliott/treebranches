Why hire Marcus Elliott?

Lifelong Computer Experience

My earliest memories do not include much programming experience beyond loading "Commander Keen" from the Command prompt or 'save-scumming' on "Castle of the Winds". But as I aged my technical capabilities became inherent.

I got my first taste of code as an eighth-grader when I published my own web-comic on freewebs.com. Over the course of its three-year run I began using light HTML and Javascript.

Love of Puzzle-solving

As a young teen I became interested in emulation in order to play foreign games that never came to the states. My interest in logic first developed when I played and completed "Mario No Super Picross" in 2001.

Now I find myself enjoying the puzzle-solving aspect of Web Development by far the most, though I do have a natural, but underdeveloped talent in design.

Redstone Inspiration!

In my final year of college as a misguided Chemistry student I discovered Minecraft and soon fell in love with its "Redstone" logic system. I constructed numerous devices, most notably my programmable "Garage code door".

For years since I repeated the same sentiment, "I should have gone to school for programming. What was I thinking? Too late!" In February 2018 I realized I was wrong and started an account on Treehouse.com.
